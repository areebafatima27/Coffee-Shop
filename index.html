About Us

We pride ourselves on being a neighbourhood favourite, serving quality coffee and excellent service. Our coffee is ethically and sustainably sourced from farmers around the world. Our baristas are highly trained to provide the best experience for you.

Our Coffee

we use only the highest quality beans, handpicked by our xperts. Try out signature ouse blend or explore our rotating selection of single-origin coffees. Our brews are always fresh, smooth and rich.

House Blend

Our signature blend combines beans from Central and South America. It has bright acidity, medium body, and sweet, caramel finish.

Nitro Coffee

A fun and unique way to enjoy coffee. Our Nitro Coffee is infused with nitrogen, which creates a cereamy and velvety texture and enhances the tatse profile.

Cold Brew

Brewed cool and steeped for 12 hours, our Cold Brew delivers a smooth and refreshing experience with rich, chocolaty notes.

Pourover

A manual brewing method that extracts the most delicate flavours from the coffee. Our baristas use high-quality grinders and slow brewing proccess to deliver the perfect taste.
